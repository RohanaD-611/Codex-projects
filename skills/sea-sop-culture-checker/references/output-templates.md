## Risk Label Wording

Use these exact labels:

- Red: 高危，发布前建议改写
- Yellow: 中风险，可保留但需调整表达或落地机制
- Green: 低风险，可直接使用或轻微本土化

## English Rewrite Style

Use business-ready English:

- Prefer "should", "are expected to", "will be reviewed", "may be escalated" over harsh command language.
- Keep "must" only for compliance, safety, legal, or non-negotiable controls.
- Use "support", "alignment", "progress summary", "manager review", and "improvement plan" where they preserve the business intent.
